Stumpwizards Ham Study Guide

Cross-platform ham radio exam prep for macOS and Windows. Open this file in any modern browser.

Focus Areas

  • FCC operating responsibilities
  • Repeaters, offsets, and tones
  • Ohm's Law and power calculations
  • Antennas, feed lines, and SWR
  • HF and VHF/UHF propagation
  • Electrical and antenna safety

Study Method

  1. Read the guide once straight through.
  2. Take one exam without notes.
  3. Review only missed questions.
  4. Repeat until scores are consistently strong.
